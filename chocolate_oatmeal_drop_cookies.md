## CHOCOLATE OATMEAL DROP COOKIES

### Ingredients

* | 1/2 c.  | milk
* | 2 c.    | sugar
* | 1 stick | butter
* | 4 Tbsp. | cocoa
* | 1/2 c.  | peanut butter
* | 3 c.    | oatmeal

---

### Directions

1. Bring milk, sugar, butter, cocoa to a boil for 1 minute
1. remove from heat and add peanut butter and oatmeal
