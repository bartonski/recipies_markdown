## Pork Chops in OJ

### Ingredients

* | 4 medium | pork chops
* | 1 clove  | garlic
* | 1 c.     | orange juice

---

### Directions

1. Marinate pork chops in orange juice overnight (ziplock bag works well for this)
1. Season pork chops with minced garlic or garlic powder
1. Grill pork chops.

---

* Serve with salad
