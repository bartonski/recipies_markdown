## crockpot barbecue chicken

### Ingredients

* | 1 whole    | chicken (cut up)
* | 1          | onion (chopped)
* | 1 1/2 cups | cola
* | 3/4 cup    | ketchup
* | 3 Tbsp.    | barbecue sauce
* | 3 Tbsp.    | brown sugar
* | 2 Tbsp.    | pepper jelly
* | 2 tsp.     | malt vinegar
* | 1/4 tsp.   | pepper

---

### Directions

1. Put in Crockpot in order listed.
1. Stir once.
1. Cover and cook on low 7 to 9 hours.
1. Remove skin and bones.
1. Serve with French fries and Cole slaw
